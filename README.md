# DriveScore 🚗

> **Know what it's really like to own a car before you buy it.**

DriveScore is an AI-powered ownership advisor that combines structured automotive data with generative AI to help people make confident car-buying decisions.

Instead of overwhelming users with specifications, reviews, and ratings, DriveScore generates a personalized ownership report based on the car they're considering and how they actually drive.

---

## Why DriveScore?

Buying a car is one of the biggest financial decisions most people make, yet the information available today is fragmented across reviews, videos, forums, and spec sheets.

DriveScore answers a much simpler question:

> **"What will it actually be like to own this car?"**

By combining deterministic ownership calculations with AI-generated explanations, DriveScore delivers personalized insights that are easy to understand and relevant to each driver.

---

## How it Works

1. Tell us about yourself

   * City
   * Daily commute
   * Ownership duration
   * Driving habits

2. Select the car

   * Brand
   * Model
   * Variant
   * Fuel type
   * Transmission

3. DriveScore analyzes

   * Fuel costs
   * Maintenance
   * Reliability
   * E20 compatibility
   * Depreciation
   * Resale value
   * Total ownership cost

4. Receive an AI-generated ownership report tailored to your driving profile.

---

## Guiding Principles

* **AI explains. It doesn't calculate.**
* Every numerical value comes from deterministic business logic.
* Reports should feel like advice from an experienced car expert.
* Simplicity over information overload.
* Build trust through transparency.
